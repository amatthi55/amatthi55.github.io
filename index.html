Latitude

The purpose of this dashboard is to explore how latitude affects weather. Over five-hundred cities in the world were randomly selected based on their latitude and then represented as points in two-dimensional graphs. The graphs on this dashboard show how cloudiness, wind speed, humidity, and temperature are affected by latitude.

The city and geographical coordinate data were collected from the citipy library and the weather data were taken from the openweathermap api.
